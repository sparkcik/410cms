How the Structure Works

Three instruments operate simultaneously, each serving a distinct purpose. Together they preserve the property, fund the investment, and generate income for all parties.

The remaining $170,000 sits untouched as your personal reserve. Interest-only payment on the $230,000 draw is approximately $1,382/month at the current 7.21% rate. 09 — LLC Governance and Protection

Your Control. Your Protection.

As managing member, you have final authority on all operational decisions. The operating agreement is also designed to protect you from any financial opacity or self-dealing concerns going forward.

Third-Party Property Management
All rent collected and expenses paid through a licensed, bonded property management firm. No managing member ever handles cash directly. Monthly statements distributed to all members independently by the management company.
Mandatory Quarterly Distributions
The operating agreement requires 80% of net cash flow to be distributed quarterly. This is not at any member's discretion. It is a contractual obligation enforceable by any member.
Annual CPA Financial Review
An independent CPA prepares annual financial statements and distributes them directly to all members. No member prepares their own financial reporting.
STR Platform Transparency
If operated as a short-term rental on Airbnb or VRBO, all members receive read-only dashboard access. Every booking, payout, and review is visible to all parties in real time.
Expense Caps and Disclosure
Any single expense above $2,500 requires written notice to all members. Any transaction involving a party related to any member must be disclosed before it occurs. No member loans permitted.
Camille's Final Authority
As managing member, Camille holds unilateral decision-making authority on rental strategy, capital improvements, vendor selection, and timing of any future sale. No minority member can override these decisions.
